1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nanobot by Nijas Khan (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our web-based chat system and when you communicate with us via WhatsApp.

Our service is operated from India and may be used by people in India and in other countries. We aim to follow basic privacy principles that are consistent with major data protection laws (such as the GDPR) without making any claim of formal certification or legal compliance guarantees.

By using our website nijaskhan.github.io/nanobot-website and our WhatsApp-based services, you agree that we may process your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use our chat system and WhatsApp integration, we may collect the following types of information:

  • WhatsApp message content: The text, media, and other content that you send to us via WhatsApp or through the web-based chat interface connected to WhatsApp Business Platform.
  • Contact details: Your WhatsApp phone number (including country code) and, if you choose to provide it, your name and other contact information (such as email address).
  • Message and usage metadata: Information such as message timestamps, message IDs, delivery and read status, technical logs, IP address, device or browser information, and general usage patterns (for example, which features are used and how often).
  • Support and feedback information: Information you share with us when you contact support, submit feedback, or communicate with us in any other way.

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary to operate our services, provide support, improve our systems, and meet our legal obligations.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Providing and operating the service: To send and receive messages via WhatsApp, operate the web-based chat interface, manage conversations, and deliver the features you use.
  • Automation and customer support: To power automated flows, chatbots, or templates, and to allow our team to respond to your questions or requests.
  • Improving and analyzing our service: To monitor performance, understand how the service is used, troubleshoot issues, and develop new features or enhancements.
  • Security and abuse prevention: To detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents, and to protect our users and systems.
  • Legal and compliance purposes: To comply with applicable laws and regulations, respond to lawful requests from authorities, and enforce our terms of use or other agreements.

We do not sell your personal data to anyone and we do not share your information with third-party advertising networks for targeted advertising.

When you communicate with us via WhatsApp:

5. Data Storage & Security

Our backend systems (for example, our Node.js server and databases) may store your message content, phone number, and related metadata in order to provide automation, support, analytics, and service improvements.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect this information, such as:

  • Storing data on secure servers with restricted access.
  • Using encryption in transit (for example, HTTPS) where appropriate.
  • Applying role-based access controls so that only authorized personnel can access user data for legitimate business purposes.
  • Monitoring systems for unusual or suspicious activity, within reasonable limits for a startup environment.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. While we work to protect your information, we cannot promise or guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including:

  • Operating and improving our services and internal systems.
  • Maintaining conversation history where needed for support, quality control, or continuity.
  • Meeting legal, accounting, or reporting obligations.
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or misuse of our services.

Where possible, we may anonymize or aggregate data so that it can no longer be linked to you personally. We may keep such anonymized or aggregated data for longer periods for analytics and business planning.

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have certain rights in relation to your personal data. We aim, in a practical and reasonable way, to respect the following types of rights:

  • Access: You can ask us whether we process your personal data and request a copy of the information we hold about you, to the extent we can reasonably retrieve it.
  • Correction: You can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information about you.
  • Objection or restriction: In some cases, you may be able to object to or request that we limit certain types of processing, especially where we rely on legitimate interests.

These rights are subject to limitations under applicable law and to our need to keep certain information (for example, for legal, security, or operational reasons). We do not claim full GDPR or other regulatory certification, but we will make good-faith efforts to respond to reasonable requests.

8. Data Deletion Request Process

You can request deletion of your personal data that we hold in connection with the WhatsApp-based services and web-based chat. To do this, please send an email to nijaskhan150@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”.

Your email should include at least the following details:

  • Your full name (if known to us).
  • Your WhatsApp phone number (including country code).
  • A short description of your request (for example, delete all chat history associated with your number).

Once we receive your request, we will review it and take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify your data from our active systems, unless we are required or permitted to keep it for legal, security, or operational reasons (for example, to comply with record-keeping requirements or to prevent fraud).

Deletion from backups and archives may take additional time, but we will aim not to use your data for active processing once your request has been confirmed.
