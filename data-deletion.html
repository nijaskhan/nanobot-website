User Data Deletion

How to request deletion of your data for NanoBot by Nijas Khan, including WhatsApp-based chat data.

Last updated: December 17, 2025

1. Overview

NanoBot by Nijas Khan is a small, experimental AI assistant that can interact with you via WhatsApp and other simple interfaces. In the course of providing this service, limited information such as your messages and phone number may be processed as described in our Privacy Policy.

If you no longer want us to keep your data, you can request deletion at any time. This page explains how to do that and what it means.

2. How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your data associated with NanoBot and our WhatsApp-based services, please send an email to:

Email: nijaskhan150@gmail.com

For faster processing, please include the following details in your message:

  • Your full name (if you have shared it with us).
  • Your WhatsApp phone number, including country code (for example, +91xxxxxxxxxx).
  • A short description of your request, such as: “Please delete all data associated with my WhatsApp number from NanoBot.”

3. What We Delete

Once we receive your request and are able to verify it, we will take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify:

  • Message content stored in our systems that is associated with your WhatsApp number.
  • Your WhatsApp phone number and any other contact details you provided directly to us.
  • Related usage logs that can reasonably be linked back to you, unless we are required to keep some records for legal, security, or operational reasons (for example, to prevent abuse or fraud).

After your request has been processed, we will not use your data for any new processing activities connected to NanoBot.

4. Limitations & Backups

While we will delete data from our active systems, some information may remain temporarily in encrypted backups or archives. These backups are kept for a limited time and are not used for active processing.

We may also retain some minimal records if necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws or regulatory requirements.
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse of our systems.
  • Demonstrate that we responded to your deletion request.
